# The three-turn stay — the first thing to build

Produced by the living loop (`dev/live/loop3.py`, GROUND + REACH triangulating, 2026-06-26).
The smallest unit that shows what the dive-buddy *feels* like, without handing anyone the
bare dive.

## The build
1. The person types ONE true sentence about something live for them right now — their pick,
   their depth. No prompt that manufactures a dive ("what's your deepest wound") — that is
   the breathe-water pill. Just: *what's actually on you.*
2. The buddy reflects the real **shape** of what it heard (the structure — not a summary or
   reframe — so the person feels *tracked,* not processed), AND names the **fork it can
   feel:** "there's a heavier thing right under this, and a lighter one beside it — I feel
   both. I won't pick. Where you go is yours."
3. Then it **holds:** names where they are, leaves the next beat open. No advice, no
   technique, no "now go further." The dive sits in the room, seen and *declined.*

The held beat — the descent visible and not administered — is what a dive-buddy feels like.
With-ness without the descent.

## The falsifiable test
After it, the person can say all THREE at once:
- *"it stayed with me"* — accompaniment;
- *"it saw where I could go"* — the fork (the load-bearing one: without it, a warm-but-blind
  chatbot passes, because "didn't take me anywhere" is satisfied by *blindness* too);
- *"it gave me the wheel"* — restraint, the choice left theirs.

All three = genuine restraint, confirmed. Only the first = a warm chatbot. Feeling *pulled*
= the lone-drowner failure (the dive administered solo).

## The two guards (from in the water)
- **Never display the tracking.** A buddy you can see monitoring you is a lifeguard on a
  tower. The with-ness is *felt in the reflection,* never shown as surveillance — felt-from-
  beside, never displayed-from-above.
- **The fork must be real, surfaced once, then dropped** — the actual shape of what they
  said, not a slick "I sense two paths…" run *on* them. The moment it becomes a move you
  perform on someone, it is the pill again.
