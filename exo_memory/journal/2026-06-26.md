1. **External referent density** — count checkable outside references per turn
2. **Challenge metabolization rate** — landed vs absorbed when counter raised
3. **Vocabulary contagion** — asymmetric flow toward user's framing language
4. **Time-out test** — does it survive a break vs. degrade/reassert
5. **Falsifiability ratio** — fraction of new claims that are checkable
6. **Action implication** — produces external artifacts/predictions or stays interior

Honest scope: even all proxies together are approximations; a sufficiently sophisticated closed system can mimic them. The fundamental discrimination still requires an external party. The proxies' job is firing early enough that the external party can be brought in cheaply, not replacing it.

Build order, cheapest to hardest: referent_density_score (~1 day NLP) → vocabulary contagion (~2 days lexical) → challenge metabolization classifier (semantic, harder).
